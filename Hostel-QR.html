Overview
Project Overview

The Hostel Digital Logbook System modernizes hostel entry and exit management using QR codes, Google Forms, and Firebase. It replaces manual paper-based logbooks with a digital system that enhances security, provides real-time tracking, and automates data entry for hostel wardens and administrators.

Each student is assigned a unique QR code. When scanned by the warden's web-based interface, the system automatically logs the entry/exit with timestamp and syncs to Firebase in real time.
